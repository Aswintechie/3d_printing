How It Works

Simple, Fast, Reliable

Getting your custom 3D print is straightforward. Here's the process from start to finish.

1

Send Your Request

Describe what you need — sketch, STL file, or just an idea. Fill in the quote form and I'll get back to you within a few hours.

2

Get a Quote

I'll review your request and send a clear quote including material, estimated print time, and cost. No hidden fees.

3

Print & Review

Once confirmed, I'll slice and print your model on the Bambu Lab A1. You'll get progress photos if needed.

Pickup in Pondicherry or shipping anywhere in India. Fast and safely packaged so your print arrives perfect.
